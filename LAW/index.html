About us

Discover a world of opportunity at VISTAS, where our captivating law programs ignite your passion for legal education. Choose from integrated B.A., LL.B (Hons.), B.Com. LL.B (Hons.), B.B.A., LL.B (Hons.), three-year LL.B, and one-year LL.M courses. Delve into Constitutional Law and Administrative Law or delve into the dynamic realm of Corporate and Commercial Law, guided by esteemed Bar Council of India and UGC eligibility criteria. Fuel your intellectual curiosity with our prestigious Ph.D. program in Law, unlocking ground breaking research opportunities. Join us at VISTAS and embark on an extraordinary legal journey, shaping your path to a remarkable and fulfilling career.
